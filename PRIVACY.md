Privacy Policy – Huoltokirja
Last Updated: 24.4.2026
The Huoltokirja mobile application is designed with a "Privacy First" approach. Our principle is simple: we do not want your data, and we do not collect it.
1. Data Collection
The application does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or personally identifiable information (PII). Any information you enter into the app (such as maintenance logs or vehicle details) is stored locally on your device only.
2. Third-Party Services
The application does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or libraries that track users or collect data. There are no analytics (e.g., Google Analytics), no advertising frameworks, and no external cloud storage integrations.
3. Permissions
The application may request only the minimum necessary system permissions required for its local functionality. These permissions are not used to harvest data.
4. Data Storage
All data created within the app stays on your device. If you delete the application, all data associated with it is also deleted, unless you have manually performed a local backup provided by your operating system (e.g., iCloud or Android backup).
5. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. However, since the app does not collect data, any changes will likely be related to new local features.
6. Contact Us
If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, you can reach out via the project's GitHub repository: https://github.com/terovirtanen/huoltokirja
